People and Culture

China is a big country in the middle of Asia. It is the 4th largest country in the world and it has the biggest population of any country in the world, about 1,443,251,911 people live in China. About 50 million Chinese live outside of China. About 60,00 Chinese live in Ireland.

Not everyone in China is chinese. A study showed that about 91.51% of people living in China are Chinese. 8.49% were minorities.

Many people in China belive in many different things. About 74% of China is either non-religious or practice Chinese folk belief, 16% are Buddhist, 2% are Christians, 1% are muslims and 8% belive in other religions.

Chinese culture is one of the world's oldest cultures, tracing back to thousands of years ago. Important components of Chinese culture includes ceramics, architecture, music, literature, martial arts, cuisine, visual arts, philosophy and religion.
