Economy and Military

China slso has a lot of money. According to the world bank, China has the largest economy in the world, totalling to 90 trillion Yuan, or 11,610,985,833,000 euro. China's economy is so big because a lot of people live in it. The more people a country has, the more people will be working, and the more money the country gets. It is one of the biggest sellers in the stock market, being the top seller in aluminum, magnesium, antimony, salt, talc, barite, cement, coal, fluorspar, gold, graphite, iron, steel, lead, mercury, molybdenum, phosphate rock, rare earths, tin, tungsten, bismuth and zinc, and has a lot of oil and coal as well.

China has a lot of rich people. China has the most amount of Millionairs and Billionairs than any other country in the world. It has about 878 Billionairs and 3.5 million millionairs.

China has A very big military. With 2.8 million troops, It has the most troops in any army, about double of the USA, witch is in 2nd place. It has about 600 planes, 537 naval ships, 3,500 tanks and 30,000 armoured vehicles. China's military budget is huge, at over 200 billion euro per year.
